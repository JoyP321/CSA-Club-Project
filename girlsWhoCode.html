Girls Who Code

Purpose

The purpose of Girls Who Code is to promote women in STEM.

Activities

Girls Who Code visits local elementary schools to teach students how to uses Processing and introduce them to Computer Science. Additionally, they discuss women in STEM fields and other current events.

Meetings

Girls who code meets every other Monday in the Mac Lab (Room 25).

People

The clubs president is Maddie Rogers. Ms. Adams is the club supervisor.
