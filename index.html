CSA Clubs!

About

CSA clubs are a great way to get involved in the CSA community and take your education beyond the classroom! This year the clubs are Girls Who Code, Robotics, and Game Development! Each of these clubs provide unique opportunities for CSA students to expand their skill sets and learn more about the ever-growing world of computer science. They are a perfect way for both new and returning students to gain coding experience, get more involved in CSA, develop leadership and collaboration skills, and make new friends along the way! On this site, you will find information such club leadership and supervision, meeting times and meeting places for each club, as well as a general overview of each club's purpose and activities they are involved in. We encourage everyone to join a club and see the extent of what the CS Academy has to offer!
