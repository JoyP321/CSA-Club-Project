Game Development

Purpose

The purpose of the Game Development Club is to make video games, teach new coders to make games, and play each other's creations.

Activities

The Game Development clubs uses Scratch (block-code) and other various languages to build computer games.

Meetings

Game Development meets in Room 26 (the PC lab) Wednesdays at lunch.

People

The clubs presidents are Brandon Arriaga and Jacob Lee. Ms. Adams is the club supervisor.
