Robotics

Purpose

The purpose of the Robotics Club is to build community and learn new skills through competitive robotics.

Activities

The Robotics Club participates in the Botball Robotics Competition. To prepare for it, the clubs designs, builds, and codes robots to fulfill the requirements of the competition. In addition, the Robotics Club experiments with 3D printing and programs smaller robots called Edisons.

Meetings

The Robotics Club meets in the Robotics room at the end of the hallway in the CSA. Meetings are Wednesdays after school from 3:15-5:15.

People

The clubs presidents are Karleigh Dehlsen and Joy Patterson and Luca Caever-Corey is Vice President. Mr. Stewart is the club supervisor.
