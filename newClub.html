Starting Your Own Club

Step 1

Come up with your club idea and find some people interested in being in it.

Step 2

Ask Mrs. Adams or Mr. Johnston if it can be affiliated with the CSA, find a teacher to be your supervisor, meeting times and locations, and decide on club officers.

Step 3

Go to the Business Office and ask for the paper work to start a new club.

Step 4

Turn in the paperwork, and ASB will approve your club!
